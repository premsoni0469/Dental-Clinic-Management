Our Mission

DENTIST is on a mission to make quality dentalcare affordable and accessible for over a billion+ Indians. We believe in empowering our users

with the most accurate, comprehensive, and curated information and care, enabling them to make better dentalcare decisions.

Healthy teeth is a habit

It is the journey that takes you to new destinations every day with endless possibilities of life on the back of happiness, energy, and hope. DENTIST wants to make this journey easy for every Indian and help them live healthier and longer lives with healthy teeth.

Our approach to dentalcare

Providing high-quality, trusted, and accessible dentalcare is our reason for being

Connect

We understand dentalcare goes beyond signs, symptoms, diagnosis, and treatment. It's about the deep connection between doctors and patients that leads to continuous care and sustained, better outcomes.

Trust

DINTIST works on trust. We are aware of the responsibility placed on us by patients. We always have and always will do everything we possibly can to uphold this trust.

Transparency

We believe in full disclosure. We believe in communicating openly and honestly, and holding ourselves to the highest ethical standards
